## Bio section

The story starts in 2024 when we noticed that most of what got called "growth"
was really just acceleration without direction. The newsletter is the response.

## Philosophy section

We're not against tools. We're against the assumption that more tools makes
better humans. Our premise: in a world where attention is the commodity, the
discipline to choose what NOT to do is the only sustainable advantage.

The newsletter ships weekly. Brief. Concrete. Made for people who already know
what they want and need help saying no to everything else.
