# Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-05-20.

## What we collect

When you book a call via the site:

- Name + email + time zone (you provide these in the Cal.com booking form)
- Booking metadata (event type, time, status) — handled by Cal.com on our behalf
- Browser locale and approximate region — Cal.com uses these to display the booking widget in your language and detect your time zone

We do NOT collect:
- Payment information (sessions are free; no payment processor involved)
- Calendar contents (Cal.com reads my availability; it doesn't read yours)
- Behavioral analytics (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no first-party tracking on this site)

## Who processes your data

Cal.com (https://cal.com) is the data processor for booking data. Cal.com's own privacy policy applies to the booking transaction; see https://cal.com/privacy.

Google Calendar is used to write confirmed bookings to my calendar. Google's privacy policy applies to that calendar event.

Google Meet is used for the video call itself. Google's privacy policy applies to call metadata (timing, duration).

## Your rights

Under GDPR (EU customers) and equivalent regimes:

- Request a copy of your data (email me; I'll fetch from Cal.com within 30 days)
- Request deletion (Cal.com booking cancellation removes the booking record from Cal.com)
- Object to processing (don't book a call — there's no data to process)

Contact: privacy@soloconsultant.example.com.
