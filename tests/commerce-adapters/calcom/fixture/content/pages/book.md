# Book a call

Pick a time that works for you. All first sessions are **free** — we'll cover what you're working on, where you're stuck, and whether I'm a fit. Meeting takes place on Google Meet — link arrives in your confirmation email.

## What to expect

- **30-min Intro Consult** — orientation call. Best if you haven't worked with me before and want to gauge fit.
- **1-hour Coaching Session** — deeper working session. Best if you've already framed the problem and want focused thinking time on it.
