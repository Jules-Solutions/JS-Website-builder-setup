# Cancellation Policy

## Free intro consults + first coaching session

These sessions are free. Cancel or reschedule any time up to **12 hours** before the meeting via the link in your confirmation email. No charge applies.

## Future paid engagements (when applicable)

This site currently offers free first sessions. If we move into ongoing engagement, separate Terms of Service will apply — sent before any paid commitment begins.

## No-show policy

If you don't attend a booked session and didn't cancel beforehand, you'll receive one rescheduling courtesy email. Repeated no-shows mean I'll deprioritize new bookings from your address.

## Contact

Reach me via the email in your confirmation, or via [Book a Call](/book) (re-book with a note).
