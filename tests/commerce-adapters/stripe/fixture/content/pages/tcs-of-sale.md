# Terms and Conditions of Sale

These Terms govern the sale of courses and consulting services by Still Humans (the "Seller") to customers (the "Buyer"). Last updated 2026-05-20.

## 1. Services

The Seller offers:

- **Cohort-based courses** (one-time purchase, fixed-duration delivery)
- **Consulting subscriptions** (recurring quarterly billing; cancellable at any time per [Refund Policy](/legal/refund-policy))

## 2. Pricing and payment

All prices are displayed in CHF (default for Swiss customers) or EUR (for EU customers); applicable VAT/sales tax handled per Section 5.

Accepted payment methods:

- **CHF transactions:** TWINT, Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal
- **EUR transactions:** Visa, Mastercard, SEPA Direct Debit, Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal

Payment is processed via Stripe (cards, TWINT, Apple Pay, Google Pay, SEPA) and PayPal. The Seller does not store payment card details.

## 3. Delivery

Course access is delivered electronically within 5 minutes of payment confirmation; consulting booking links delivered within 5 minutes. If delivery fails, contact support@stillhumans.example.ch.

See [Refund Policy](/legal/refund-policy) for full terms.

The Seller is the Merchant of Record. The Seller is responsible for collecting and remitting applicable VAT (EU/EEA customers) or other regional taxes per jurisdiction. Invoices include tax breakdown where applicable.

## 6. Governing law

These Terms are governed by Swiss law. Jurisdiction: courts of Zurich, Switzerland.
