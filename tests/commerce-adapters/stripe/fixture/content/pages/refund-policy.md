# Refund Policy

## One-time course purchases

We offer a **14-day full refund** from the date of purchase, no questions asked. If you've started the cohort and decided the program isn't a fit, email us at refunds@stillhumans.example.ch with your order ID; we'll process the refund within 5 business days.

## Recurring consulting subscriptions

You may cancel your quarterly subscription at any time via the [Customer Portal](/account). Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current paid quarter; you retain access until that date. We do not pro-rate refunds for partial quarters.

## Refund processing

Refunds are returned to the original payment method:

- **Card payments:** 5-10 business days to appear on your statement
- **TWINT (CHF):** typically 3-5 business days back to your linked bank account
- **PayPal:** 1-3 business days to your PayPal balance

For any questions, contact refunds@stillhumans.example.ch.
