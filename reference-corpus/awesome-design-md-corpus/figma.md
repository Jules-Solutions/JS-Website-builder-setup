# Figma — DESIGN.md

**Personality:** Playful-professional, colorful, energetic. Celebrates creativity — multi-color brand marks, bold shapes, white canvas with vivid pops. Confident and fun without being childish.

## Color
- Canvas: clean white `#FFFFFF` (the "canvas" metaphor) + light grays.
- Brand multicolor: red `#F24E1E`, orange `#FF7262`, purple `#A259FF`, blue `#1ABCFE`, green `#0ACF83` — the five-color logo palette, used as energetic accents/blobs.
- Text: near-black `#1E1E1E` + grays.
- **Take-away:** white canvas + a curated *multi*-color accent set (rare — most brands use one) = creative energy.

## Typography
- Sans-serif, geometric-friendly (Figma uses "Whyte"). Bold confident headlines; clean body.
- Big, punchy marketing type; playful weight contrast.

## Layout & spacing
- Spacious, grid-aligned, with colorful shapes/illustration breaking the grid for energy.
- Medium radii (~8–12px); rounded, approachable. Lots of product-canvas imagery.

## Components
- Buttons: bold fills (often the brand purple/blue), rounded, clear. Friendly + confident.
- Cards: white, soft shadow, colorful accents, comfortable padding.
- Signature: multi-color brand blobs, canvas/cursor motifs, collaborative multiplayer cursors.

## Motion
- Lively but smooth — playful transforms, cursor/collaboration animation. Energetic, not frantic.

## Voice (microcopy)
- Friendly, enthusiastic, creative-community tone. Inclusive and encouraging.

## Signature moves
- White canvas + five-color accent palette + bold geometric sans + rounded shapes = "Figma."

## When to reference this
Briefs wanting **playful-professional, colorful, creative, energetic** — design tools, creative communities, collaborative products, anything that should feel fun *and* capable.
