# Spotify — DESIGN.md

**Personality:** Bold, dark, vibrant, music-forward. Black canvas that makes album art and energetic color pop. Confident, youthful, expressive — built around imagery and rhythm.

## Color
- Brand: Spotify green — `#1DB954` / `#1ED760` (brighter current). The unmistakable signal.
- Canvas: near-black — `#121212` / `#000000`. Dark by default.
- Text: white `#FFFFFF` + light grays `#B3B3B3`.
- Accents: vivid duotones + per-playlist gradient color extracted from cover art.
- **Take-away:** black canvas + electric green + art-driven gradient color = "Spotify."

## Typography
- **Spotify Circular** — geometric rounded sans. Bold, friendly, modern. Big confident headings.
- Heavy weight contrast; type is loud in marketing, clean in product.

## Layout & spacing
- Image-led grids (album/playlist art everywhere); generous on marketing, dense in product.
- Medium radii (~8px on cards, fully-round on play buttons). Bold, energetic.

## Components
- Buttons: green pill primary (the play button is iconic), white/ghost secondary. Round, bold.
- Cards: dark surface, large cover image, hover-reveal play. Art is the hero.
- Signature: the green play button, duotone editorial imagery, art-extracted gradient backgrounds.

## Motion
- Energetic, rhythmic, smooth. Playful reveals + scroll energy; equalizer/pulse motifs.

## Voice (microcopy)
- Bold, playful, culturally-fluent, expressive. Confident and contemporary.

## Signature moves
- `#121212` black + `#1DB954` green + Circular + big cover art + duotone gradients = "Spotify."

## When to reference this
Briefs wanting **bold, dark, vibrant, image-led, youthful** — media, music/audio, entertainment, culture brands, anything that should feel energetic and art-forward.
