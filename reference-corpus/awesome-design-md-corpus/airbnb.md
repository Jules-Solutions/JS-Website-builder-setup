# Airbnb — DESIGN.md

**Personality:** Warm, welcoming, human, photography-led. The "belong anywhere" feeling — rounded, soft, generous imagery, a friendly coral accent. Design recedes so the places (photos) shine.

## Color
- Brand: "Rausch" coral-red — `#FF5A5F`. Warm, inviting, the signature accent.
- Canvas: white `#FFFFFF` + light warm grays.
- Text: near-black `#222222` (Airbnb's "Hof" dark) + `#717171` secondary.
- **Take-away:** white + photography + one warm coral accent; color stays out of the way of imagery.

## Typography
- **Airbnb Cereal** — the custom rounded-friendly sans across all weights. Approachable, clean, modern.
- Clear hierarchy; comfortable, readable body; friendly rounded letterforms.

## Layout & spacing
- Generous whitespace, card-grid layouts, large rounded imagery. Photography is the hero.
- Rounded corners (~12px on cards/images) — softness is core to "belonging."

## Components
- Buttons: coral-gradient/coral primary, white label, rounded, clear; quiet secondary.
- Cards: photo-led, rounded, minimal chrome, price + rating as quiet detail.
- Inputs/search: the rounded pill search bar is iconic. Large, friendly fields.
- Signature: full-bleed photography, rounded cards, the coral accent, the search pill.

## Motion
- Smooth, gentle, welcoming. Soft transitions; image-forward. Calm, never jarring.

## Voice (microcopy)
- Warm, human, inclusive. "Belong anywhere." Hospitable and reassuring.

## Signature moves
- Rausch coral `#FF5A5F` + Cereal + rounded cards + big photography + white canvas = "Airbnb."

## When to reference this
Briefs wanting **warm, welcoming, human, photography-led** — travel, hospitality, marketplaces, lifestyle, anything where imagery sells and the design should feel inviting.
