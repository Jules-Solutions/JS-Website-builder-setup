# Nike — DESIGN.md

**Personality:** Bold, athletic, confident, attitude-forward. Black-and-white with explosive action photography and huge, often uppercase type. Energy and movement; "Just Do It." Design with swagger.

## Color
- Core: black `#111111` and white `#FFFFFF` — the dominant, high-contrast pair.
- Accent: Nike's volt/orange used punchily on CTAs; product color comes from the imagery.
- **Take-away:** monochrome base + explosive photography + rationed loud accent = athletic power.

## Typography
- Bold condensed/grotesque (Nike uses "Trade Gothic"/"Futura" heritage). Big, tight, frequently uppercase.
- Extreme weight + scale; headlines shout. Type carries the energy.

## Layout & spacing
- Big, bold, full-bleed; large action imagery; confident asymmetry. High contrast everywhere.
- Small radii / sharp edges; aggressive cropping; motion-implied compositions.

## Components
- Buttons: black/white pill or block, uppercase bold label, fast hover. Decisive.
- Cards: product-photo-led, minimal chrome, bold price/name, edge-to-edge imagery.
- Signature: the swoosh, full-bleed athlete photography, oversized condensed headlines, "Just Do It."

## Motion
- Kinetic, punchy, fast. Implied + literal movement; energetic reveals. Athletic, never sleepy.

## Voice (microcopy)
- Bold, motivational, imperative, attitude. Short and hard-hitting. "Just Do It."

## Signature moves
- Black/white + oversized condensed uppercase + explosive action photography + the swoosh = "Nike."

## When to reference this
Briefs wanting **bold, athletic, high-energy, attitude-driven** — sports, fitness, streetwear, performance brands, anything that should feel powerful, kinetic, and confident.
