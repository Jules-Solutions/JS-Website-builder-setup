# Apple — DESIGN.md

**Personality:** Premium, minimal, product-hero. The reference for "expensive and effortless." Vast whitespace, enormous crisp type, and immaculate product photography on white or black. Restraint as luxury.

## Color
- Canvas: pure white `#FFFFFF` and rich near-black `#000000` / `#1D1D1F` — alternating full-bleed sections.
- Text: `#1D1D1F` on light, `#F5F5F7` on dark; secondary gray `#86868B`.
- Accent: `#0071E3` (Apple blue) for links/CTAs — used sparingly. Product color comes from the photography.
- **Take-away:** near-zero brand color; the products and the type *are* the design.

## Typography
- **SF Pro** (SF Pro Display for large, SF Pro Text for small). On the web, substitute Inter / system-ui.
- Huge, tight, bold marketing headlines; generous body. Extreme size contrast = drama from type alone.

## Layout & spacing
- Enormous whitespace; centered, single-focus sections; one product, one idea per screen.
- Small-to-medium radii; precise alignment. Full-bleed product imagery is the centerpiece.

## Components
- Buttons: pill-shaped blue/black, minimal, "Buy"/"Learn more." Quiet, confident.
- Cards: clean white/black, big image, short copy, lots of margin.
- Signature: scroll-driven product reveals, parallax, gigantic headlines, perfect product shots.

## Motion
- Refined, physical, scroll-choreographed. Premium and smooth; never gratuitous. Honors reduce-motion.

## Voice (microcopy)
- Spare, confident, benefit-first. Short declaratives. "The most advanced..." restrained to the essentials.

## Signature moves
- White/black alternation + huge tight type + product-hero photography + vast whitespace = "Apple."

## When to reference this
Briefs wanting **premium, minimal, product-hero, luxurious-restraint** — hardware, premium products, flagship launches, anything that should feel expensive and effortless. (On the web, substitute Inter for SF Pro.)
