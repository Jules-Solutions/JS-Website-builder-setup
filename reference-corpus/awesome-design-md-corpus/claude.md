# Claude (Anthropic) — DESIGN.md

**Personality:** Warm, literary, calm, human. Reads like paper and ink, not a dashboard. The anti-"cold AI" — approachable, considered, a little editorial.

## Color
- Background: warm off-white / soft paper — `#F5F4EF` to `#FAF9F5` (never pure `#FFF`).
- Accent: Anthropic "clay" / copper-coral — `#D97757` (warm terracotta). Used sparingly for emphasis + CTAs.
- Text: warm near-black slate — `#1F1E1D` / `#3D3C39`.
- Surfaces: layered warm grays; borders are subtle and warm-tinted.
- **Take-away:** warmth comes from tinting *every* neutral slightly toward cream/clay — no cool grays.

## Typography
- Display/serif: a refined serif (Anthropic uses Tiempos-style serif) for headings + long-form — signals "considered writing."
- Body/UI: a clean grotesque sans (Styrene-style) for interface text.
- Generous line-height; comfortable measure for reading. Type is a primary brand carrier.

## Layout & spacing
- Roomy, unhurried. Generous margins, single-column reading surfaces, restrained density.
- Soft, medium corner radii (~8–12px). Nothing sharp or aggressive.

## Components
- Buttons: clay-filled primary with warm label; quiet secondary. Soft, friendly.
- Cards/message bubbles: warm surface, soft border, comfortable padding.
- Signature: the conversation surface itself — calm, paper-like, content-first.

## Motion
- Subtle, gentle. Soft fades and eases; nothing flashy. Respects calm.

## Voice (microcopy)
- Thoughtful, plain, warm. "Helpful, harmless, honest." Avoids hype and jargon.

## Signature moves
- Cream/paper backgrounds + clay accent + serif headings = instantly "Claude."
- Warmth-by-tinting every neutral; content and writing as the hero.

## When to reference this
Briefs wanting **warm, literary, calm, human, trustworthy** — knowledge tools, writing products, considered B2C, anything that should feel the opposite of a cold SaaS dashboard.
