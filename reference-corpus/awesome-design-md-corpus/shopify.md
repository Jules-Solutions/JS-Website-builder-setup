# Shopify — DESIGN.md

**Personality:** Approachable, trustworthy, optimistic. Designed to make commerce feel possible for everyone. Green-led, friendly, confident — practical rather than precious.

## Color
- Brand: Shopify green — `#008060` / `#5C6AC4`-era indigo historically; current marketing leans rich green + black.
- Canvas: white + warm off-whites for sections.
- Text: near-black `#1A1A1A` + grays.
- Accents: green for go/commerce/trust; supportive warm illustration colors.
- **Take-away:** green = the trust/commerce signal; warm illustration keeps it human.

## Typography
- Sans-serif (Shopify uses "Shopify Sans" / Inter-like). Clear, confident, friendly headlines; readable body.
- Strong, approachable hierarchy — marketing-bold but never intimidating.

## Layout & spacing
- Generous, grid-clean, lots of breathing room. Friendly illustration + real merchant photography.
- Medium radii (~8px); soft, approachable. (Polaris, the product design system, is tighter + data-dense for the admin.)

## Components
- Buttons: green primary, white label, clear hover; plain secondary. Confident, inviting.
- Cards: white, soft shadow/border, comfortable padding, merchant imagery or illustration.
- Signature: friendly custom illustration, merchant success stories, green CTAs.

## Motion
- Smooth, welcoming, purposeful. Nothing flashy — trust over spectacle.

## Voice (microcopy)
- Encouraging, plain, empowering. "You can do this." Speaks to entrepreneurs warmly.

## Signature moves
- Shopify green + warm white + friendly illustration + confident sans = "Shopify."

## When to reference this
Briefs wanting **approachable, trustworthy, optimistic, commerce-ready** — small-business sites, e-commerce, SaaS for non-technical owners, anything that must feel encouraging and capable.
